Differential privacy often enters the story through a hypothesis-testing lens: how much can an adversary distinguish neighboring datasets after we add noise? For a Gaussian mechanism with fixed sensitivity, the feasible region in ROC space depends on the noise scale.

The self-learning DP as hypothesis testing blog post develops this picture in full. Below is the same interactive widget used in that lecture — it loads automatically after the page is ready (the first load downloads a Python runtime in the browser).
